About Page

What is this?

Navigating a huge campus like the University of Maryland can be stressful. This website is designed to help locate specific buildings to help relieve the stress and allow people to get to their final destination as easily as possible. Other filters can also be applied to find a list of buildings that satisfy the selected category and its location on the map.

How to Use It

The Home page consists of the filter options and a specific building name can be inputted followed by pressing the filter button or the checkboxes can be selected to get a list of buildings.

What is being displayed?

After the data has been filtered, the map will have markers of where the building is and there are pop up labels on each marker when clicking on it. The right side of the page is displaying the list of corresponding building names based on the filter.
